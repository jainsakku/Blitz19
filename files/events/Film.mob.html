ANIMATE

Typography animations, or simply 'Animated Text', is an interactive way to express ideas, tell stories or evoke a particular emotion. Use of 'text motion' and effects can generate interest in even the most boring of presentations. If that sounds fun to you, join us for 'ANIMATE', an online 2D typography animation challenge searching for the most impactful animations.
