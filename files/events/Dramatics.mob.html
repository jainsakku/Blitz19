RANGBHOOMI

Art, acting and performance are an expression of life - our culture, our worries, our love, our strength, our weaknesses, and our existential curiosity. Drama is the best way to give away your message to society. Are you good at the same art? ADVITIYA

Imagine an actor-less world. There would be no nighttime TV show to get us through a long working day. No topic for catch-ups with friends and empty nights filled with boredom and stares at a useless tv screen. Mono Acting is an art which deals with very delicate emotion. It is not putting up the mask but exposing your talent. So expose yourself with your story, just begin it, end it and tell everything in between through ADIVITIYA - The Mono Act Competition
